Help & troubleshooting

If captions don't start, keep disconnecting, or something feels off, use the diagnostics before changing settings at random.

Recommended steps

  • 1. Run diagnostics and check that the backend status and realtime WebSocket checks are OK.
  • 2. Check the microphone diagnostic and make sure your browser has permission to use the correct input device.
  • 3. Use "Copy diagnostics to clipboard" and paste the report when contacting LinguaLive Pro support.

The diagnostics report includes technical environment info (plan, browser, backend status) but never your audio or transcript content.
